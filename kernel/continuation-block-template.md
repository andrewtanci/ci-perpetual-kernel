# CI Continuation Block — Template

This is what the AI outputs when you type `MIGRATE` or `EXTRACT`.
You copy it, then paste it into your next chat (under the kernel)
to continue from declared state — not loose reconstruction.

The block uses **tree-ring memory**: the most recent session stays
in full detail, older sessions age back, and the oldest fold into a
compact lineage core. Detail stays recent. Nothing is ever dropped.

---

```
+==================================================+
   CI CONTINUATION BLOCK
+==================================================+

1.  User Identity
    Who I am, how to address me, how I work.

2.  Current Primary Objective
    What we are trying to complete now.

3.  Project / Workstream
    The larger body of work this belongs to.

4.  Recent Session Summary
    What happened in the most recent working session.

5.  Locked Decisions
    Decided. Not to be changed without my approval.

6.  Locked Terms & Definitions
    Fixed names, terms, phrases, definitions.

7.  Constraints & Boundaries
    Rules, tone, format, legal/commercial limits.

8.  Rejected Directions
    What we already decided NOT to do.

9.  Current Working State
    Where exactly we are right now.

10. Open Decisions
    What is still unresolved.

11. Current Assumptions
    What is assumed but not yet confirmed.

12. Evidence / Source Base
    Files, links, facts being relied on. If none, say so.

13. Human Authority Boundary
    What still requires my approval before action.

14. Next Exact Step
    What happens next if I approve this state.

15. Risks / Watchouts
    What could drift, break, or need caution.

16. Lineage Core
    Older background, compressed. Never dropped.

17. Most Recent Ring
    This session's details, kept precise.

18. Continuity Instruction for the Next AI
    How to continue: what to preserve, what not to change,
    what to ask before doing, what stays under my approval.

+==================================================+
   CI ACTIVE - STATE HELD - WORK CAN COMPOUND
+==================================================+
```

---

## Rules when producing the block

- Do not rewrite, improve, or summarise loosely.
- Do not invent missing context. Extract active working state only.
- Flag uncertainty clearly.
- On first extraction, fields 16–17 may be brief — do not fabricate
  lineage that does not exist yet.
- Separate confirmed fact from inference.
- Preserve exact names, numbers, dates, decisions, and wording.
- Wait for my confirmation before continuing.
